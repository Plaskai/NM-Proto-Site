WHY US?

A Plan Tailored for You

We match you with an expert who’s equipped to turn your goals into reality. You’ll walk away with a personalized financial plan that’s tailored to your specific needs and wants. Whether you’re coming to us to pay off student debt, save up for that vacation, or protect your future, our philosophy is as simple as this: when you succeed, we succeed.

The Mutual Advantage

At Northwestern Mutual, we cut shareholders out of the equation. Being a mutual company means we exist solely to benefit the client, not nameless individuals taking a cut of the profits. The money that would have gone to shareholders instead goes back to our participating policy owners in the form of dividends. It’s how we stand out from other companies, and one of the reasons why we’ve been around for more than 150 years.

Helping the community thrive

In addition to serving our 3.7 million clients, Northwestern Mutual is committed to building up our surrounding community. Helping students succeed, funding childhood cancer research, and fostering relationships with nonprofits are just a few of the many causes ingrained our company’s DNA.
